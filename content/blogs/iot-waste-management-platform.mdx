Waste management becomes a data problem when bins can report fill level, battery state, location, and sensor health. The useful product is not the raw signal; it is a prioritized collection decision.

## Ingest telemetry defensively

Every message is validated, timestamped, and associated with a device identity. Out-of-order readings are expected, so I retain event time separately from ingestion time.

## Convert readings into actions

Thresholds are only a starting point. A route should consider predicted fill level, location, collection windows, and whether a device has gone silent.

## Design for imperfect devices

Devices go offline and sensors drift. Monitoring last-seen time, battery trends, and improbable changes gives operations staff enough context to trust the dashboard.
